1. Data Collection: "The Vault"

We collect audio data only when a "Recall" event is manually triggered by the user via a hardware or software gesture. The 45-second pre-trigger buffer is stored temporarily in your device's RAM (volatile memory) and is overwritten continuously. This buffer is never uploaded to our servers unless a capture is initiated by the user.

2. Use of Audio and Transcription Data

Captured audio is processed for transcription purposes using secure third-party AI providers. We do not sell, rent, or lease your voice data to third parties. Your "Recall Tags" (audio and text) are encrypted and accessible only by you through your authenticated account.

3. Necessary Permissions

To function as an "Active-Workflow" tool, the App requires the following permissions:

Microphone: To maintain the local retrospective buffer.
Bluetooth: To communicate with peripherals for hardware-based triggering.
Background Refresh: To ensure the buffer remains active when the device is locked or the App is not in the foreground.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption protocols (AES-256) to protect data in "The Vault." Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices through biometrics (FaceID/TouchID) or strong passcodes.

5. Australian Privacy Principles (APP)

We comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). You have the right to access your personal data, request corrections, or request the full deletion of your account and associated data at any time via the App settings.

6. Contact Information

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact: info@recallpoint.com.au
